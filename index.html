DermaScan

AI skin-lesion classifier as a regulated SaMD

An AI melanoma-screening classifier taken through the full EU MDR SaMD process as a simulation: Rule 11 classification (Class IIa), an ISO 14971 risk file with an AI-hazard register, an IEC 62304 (Class C) software lifecycle, EU AI Act Annex IV documentation, clinical evaluation, and a post-market KPI dashboard, plus a FastAPI inference service.

MedVis — Needle Insertion Planning

Medical visualization + systems engineering

A 3D Slicer Python module that plans a percutaneous needle trajectory on a CT volume and checks it against a segmented no-go vessel, paired with an Arcadia/Capella MBSE model, an IREB requirements spec with a traceability matrix, and an ISO 14971 hazard analysis, one thread from clinical hazard down to the line of code and its unit test.

QMS Internal Audit — Telemedicine App

ISO 9001:2015 QMS design & internal audit

A quality-management case study: scoping and internally auditing a QMS for a telemedicine mobile app against ISO 9001:2015, with the audit aligned to ISO 19011:2018. Covers regulatory qualification (MDR Rule 11 / SaMD), SIPOC and Turtle process maps, a scored risk register, a graded findings register (Major/Minor NC, OFI), and a phased CAPA improvement roadmap.
